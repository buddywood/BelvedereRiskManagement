# Belvedere Risk Management

## What This Is

A web application that converts structured Family Governance risk intake into scored assessments, recommended mitigations, and auto-generated client deliverables (PDF reports + policy templates). Think "TurboTax for family risk" - a guided 12-minute interview that helps high-net-worth families identify and address governance risks before they become family conflicts.

## Core Value

Prevent family wealth from becoming family conflict through systematic risk assessment and actionable governance recommendations.

## Context

**Service Model**: This is a new service offering targeting high-net-worth families who need structured governance to prevent wealth-related conflicts.

**User Experience**: 12-minute guided assessment that feels conversational rather than form-based. Smart branching logic skips irrelevant questions (no trusts = no trustee questions, no kids = no succession planning).

**Risk Categories**: Family Governance covers decision-making authority, access controls, trust/estate governance, marriage/relationship risk, succession planning, behavior standards, business involvement, and documentation gaps.

**Deliverables**: Immediate results showing pillar score + top 3 risks + recommended actions, plus downloadable branded report and pre-filled governance policy templates.

## Constraints

- **Tech Stack**: Full-stack JavaScript — consistent language throughout
- **Timeline**: 2-4 weeks for working MVP demo — aggressive launch timeline
- **Budget**: Minimal hosting costs — avoid expensive services, simple deployment
- **Scope**: Family Governance pillar only — other risk areas deferred to future versions
